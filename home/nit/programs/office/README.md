# Office

This module configures the user's productivity and office applications. It allows working with documents, notes and calculations without relying on cloud services.

## Files

### `default.nix`
When the profile is enabled, installs:

- **Obsidian**: note-taking app with connected notes that allows creating a personal knowledge base with links between notes. Chosen for its ability to create knowledge networks and its Markdown-based format.
- **sticky-notes**: quick notes app for temporary reminders directly on the desktop.
- **SpeedCrunch**: desktop calculator with history, arbitrary precision and advanced syntax. Preferred over basic calculators for its power and speed.
- **OnlyOffice Desktop Editors**: office suite compatible with Microsoft Office formats (DOCX, XLSX, PPTX). Included for editing Office documents natively without relying on Google Docs or LibreOffice.
- **Readest**: e-book reader that supports formats like EPUB and PDF. Allows reading digital books comfortably from the desktop.

These applications cover basic office and study needs while keeping data local and private.
